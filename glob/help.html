Help

roshambo requires two active players.

You'll know the game is successfully in progress if a yellow countdown timer appears.

Make sure your choice is set before the timer runs out.

You can change your choice any time before the timer runs out.

You should get the result of your game within a few seconds of that time, otherwise something has gone wrong.

For more info: click here (about).
