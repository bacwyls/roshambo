Help

roshambo requires two active players.

Find someone to play with, enter their name, and decide on a time when you will both SHOOT.

Then, press SHOOT at around the same time.

You'll know the game is successfully in progress if a timer pops up in your game status.

You can change your shoot choice any time before the timer runs out.

You should get the result of your game within a few seconds of that time, otherwise something has gone wrong.

For more info: click here (about).
