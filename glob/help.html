Help

roshambo requires two active players.

Find someone to play with, enter their name, and decide on a time when you will both SHOOT.

If you both press SHOOT at around the same time, results should appear shortly thereafter.

You'll know the game is successfully in progress if a time string pops up in your game status.

You should get the result of your game within a few seconds of that time string, otherwise something has gone wrong.

For more info: click here (about).

If you're still having problems, contact ~bacwyl-samweg or uninstall the app by entering:

|uninstall %roshambo

in your dojo.
